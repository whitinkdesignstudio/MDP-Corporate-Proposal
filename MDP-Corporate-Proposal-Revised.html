Content Strategy

Four Content Pillars That
Build the Brand

Every piece of content serves a clear strategic purpose. We operate across four focused pillars — each targeting a distinct audience segment while compounding brand equity over the 3-month period.

☕ The Coffee Culture Pillar
Reels and posts celebrating the ritual of filter coffee — steam, aroma, the brass tumbler. Emotional connection content that drives shares, saves, and cultural affinity for the MDP brand.
🏢 The Corporate Trust Pillar
Office testimonials, client office tours, reactions from employees at Amazon, Microsoft, Wipro. "Who drinks MDP?" social proof storytelling that speaks directly to decision-makers at target companies.
👨‍🍳 The Behind-The-Cup Pillar
Training academy content, sourcing stories, how the filter is made, staff spotlights. Builds authenticity and transparency — the kind of brand narrative that earns long-term corporate loyalty.
🎯 The B2B Acquisition Pillar
LinkedIn thought leadership, case studies, HR & facility manager targeted posts and direct outreach. "Does your office have a coffee problem?" awareness campaigns driving traffic to our Partner With Us landing page.
